Feature Engineering Explanation
Why Feature Engineering Matters

Feature engineering is the process of using domain knowledge to create new variables that make machine learning algorithms work better. Here's why each feature was created:

  • rooms_per_household: Measures housing density and space available per household, which is likely to correlate with housing prices.
  • bedrooms_ratio: Indicates the proportion of rooms dedicated to bedrooms, which may reflect property type and layout.
  • population_per_household: Captures household size, possibly indicating family demographics in the area.
  • income_per_capita: Adjusts income for household size, giving a better measure of economic well-being.
  • distance_to_ocean: A rough approximation of distance from the coast, since coastal properties in California often command higher prices.
